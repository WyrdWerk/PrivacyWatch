# Legal Quick Guide - Nebius Token Factory documentation

## 1. Introduction: Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Nebius Token Factory, your trust is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your data, respecting your privacy, and operating in full compliance with applicable laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

## 2. Data Processing: What Happens to Your Data

**2.1. Data Controller:** For your account information, we act as the Data Controller.
**2.2. Data Processor:** For content submitted via API, we act as the Data Processor under our DPA. You are the Data Controller.
**2.3. Speculative Decoding:** To improve output speed, your prompts and outputs may be stored and used for Speculative Decoding. You can switch this off by enabling Zero Data Retention.

## 3. Your Controls: Data Retention & Opt-Outs

**3.1. Zero Data Retention (ZDR):** When enabled, inputs and outputs are not stored after request processing. This data is never used for model training.
**3.2. Default behavior:** Unless ZDR is enabled, inputs and outputs are kept for Speculative Decoding.

## 4. Data Location: Where Your Data is Processed

We offer models hosted in the EU (Finland, France), Israel, and the United States. The hosting location is clearly indicated for each model.

**Fine-Tuning Data:** All fine-tuning data is stored exclusively in EU data centers, regardless of processing location.

## 5. Compliance & Certifications

- GDPR compliant
- DPA incorporated into Terms of Service (no separate signature needed)
- ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2 Type II
- HIPAA support available
- EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified

## 6. Ownership & Copyright

You retain all ownership rights to your inputs, outputs, and fine-tuned models.

## 7. FAQ

**Is my data used to train your AI models?** No, we don't use your data to train any AI models. Your data is used only for speculative decoding, if you do not enable ZDR.
**Do I need to sign a separate DPA?** No. Our DPA is an integral part of our Terms of Service.
