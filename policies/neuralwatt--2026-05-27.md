# Privacy Policy | Neuralwatt Cloud

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
Name, email address, company name — collected when creating account, contacting support, registering for services.

### Usage Data
API usage statistics, model performance metrics, energy consumption data, billing and usage information, feature usage patterns.

### Technical Information
IP addresses, browser type, device info, operating system, referring URLs.

### Inference Data
- **Prompts and completions:** Processed transiently, **not permanently stored**. Conversation context held in volatile memory for up to 24 hours, then purged.
- **Semantic representations:** Anonymized numerical representations may be retained for cache performance.
- Token counts for billing, model selection, performance metrics.

## How We Use Your Information

Provide and improve services, process requests, calculate billing (energy + token usage), optimize performance and energy efficiency, communicate, monitor trends, detect issues, protect security, comply with legal obligations.

## Data Sharing

- Service Providers (payment, cloud, analytics)
- Legal Compliance
- Business Transfers
- With Your Consent

## No Training on Your Data

**We do not use your API inputs or outputs to train, fine-tune, or improve any machine learning models. Your inference data is yours.**

## Data Retention

- **Inference content:** Real-time processing, not retained beyond 24-hour operational caching
- **Billing/usage records:** Duration of account + legal requirements
- **Analytics:** Up to 90 days
- **Account info:** Until deletion requested
