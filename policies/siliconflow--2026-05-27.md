# Privacy Policy - SiliconFlow

Welcome to use the high-value GenAI platform of SILICONFLOW TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD. ("SiliconFlow" or "we","us", or "our"). We attach great importance to the protection of Personal Data from users ("you").

## 1. Definitions

- "Data Protection Laws" means all applicable laws relating to collection, disclosure, use and/or Processing of Personal Data, including PDPA.
- "PDPA" means the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 of Singapore.
- "Personal Data" means data about an individual who can be identified from that data.

## 2. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Data

### 2.1 Information Provided by You
- Register and Log in via third-party platforms (Google, GitHub)
- Subscribe and Purchase Services (credit card, bank account, billing info via Stripe)
- Surveys and Online Events
- Contract-related Information

### 2.2 Information We Automatically Collect
- Security and Operation (device info, network access, web browsing records)
- Service Improvement (network logs, frequency of use, crash data)
- Troubleshooting (consultation records, communication records)
- Notification and Marketing

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- Affiliated companies and partners may share your information with us

## 3. Interaction Data

**Interaction Data** includes data generated through the Services including input, feedback, modification, processing, storage, uploading, downloading, distribution. You are responsible for storing your Interaction Data. We only provide storage services as required by law.

## 4. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies to collect and store account information, search history, and login status. You can accept or decline cookies by modifying browser settings.

## 5. How We Share Your Personal Data

- Our Affiliates
- Service Providers (payment, customer service, analytics)
- Governmental and regulatory authorities
- Buyer or successor of our business
- Our advisors

## 7. How We Retain Your Personal Data

We retain Personal Data as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law.

## 8. Your Rights

- Withdrawing consent
- Accessing and correcting Personal Data (reasonable fee may apply for access)
- Response within 30 days

## 9. Protection

We take appropriate administrative, physical, and technical measures.
