# Wafer Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Wafer, Inc. operates an inference platform that serves optimized open-source large language models via API.

## 1. Scope

Applies to: wafer.ai website, inference API (Wafer Pass, dedicated/enterprise endpoints), account/billing interactions.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account and billing data
Name, email, billing information. Payments processed by payment provider; we don't store full credit card numbers.

### 2.2 API request data
- Prompts, completions, tool/function call payloads
- Request metadata: model, timestamp, token counts, latency, IP, user agent, API key identifier
- Error traces and diagnostic information

### 2.3 Website data
Standard web analytics, cookies for site operation and basic analytics.

## 3. How We Use Data

- Serve inference requests
- Operate, monitor, improve reliability/performance/security
- Investigate abuse, fraud, ToS violations
- Bill for usage and manage accounts
- Communicate about account, service changes, product updates (opt-in)

## 4. Model Training

**We do not train the models we serve on your prompts or completions.** Inputs and outputs are never used to train, fine-tune, or improve served models, and not shared with third-party model providers for training.

**Speculative decoding:** We train small internal "draft" models used only for speculative decoding to accelerate inference. Draft models are used internally, not exposed as a product, not shared with third parties. Traffic covered by Zero Data Retention is excluded from draft-model training.

## 5. Data Retention

**Standard offerings:** Request logs may be stored in anonymized form for up to 30 days for abuse detection, then deleted.

**Wafer Pass Privacy (ZDR):** Prompts and completions are not written to durable storage. Only operational metadata retained.

## 6. Wafer Pass Privacy - Zero Data Retention

- Prompts and completions not written to durable storage after request processing
- Not used for speculative-decoding draft-model training
- Only minimal metadata retained (token counts, timestamps, request IDs, model, latency)

## 7. Sharing and Disclosure

- Infrastructure providers under contractual confidentiality terms
- Payment and business tools (limited data)
- Legal requests when required
- Business transfers (merger/acquisition)

**We do not sell personal data or share prompts/completions with advertisers or data brokers.**

## 8. Security

TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest, access controls, audit logging.

## 10. International Users

Inference served from US data centers. Data transferred to and processed in the US.
